Audience

The audience for this website will be small business owners and customers

Small Business Owners

Scenarios:

  • Motivated entrepreneurs who are in need of networking to get their business started
  • Business owners needing a jump start after their success has plateaued or decreased

Customers

Scenarios:

  • Customers who want to learn more about their local businesses
  • People who are interested in public events hosted by companies
  • Those who are currently looking for a job can connect with their local businesses for employment
